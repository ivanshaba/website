PURPOSE

VISION

To put in place the best sustainable circus programs and acts through edutainment performances that impact the communities with increasing awareness, providing employment opportunities, birthing and nurturing talent through the circus.

MISSION

Our mission is to be the most impacting in communities with our circus edutainment and create fun-filled moments and employment opportunities for circus artists in slums to shine through exposure, training, and empowerment.

VALUES

God fearing, hardworking, teamwork, discipline, honesty and determination.

ACTIVITIES

Entertainment services for parties, events, and weddings, co-operateevents, we offer advertisements and do out reaches with organizations and awareness programs in schools and communities.

AIM

To build the best teams and groups of circus artists who are financially able to sustain their lives as well as impact communities positively through circus entertainment.

MAIN FOCUS

To boost the arts industry through circus entertainment, create employment opportunities for youths,empower youth with skills and transform lives and communities.
